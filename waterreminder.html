Daily Water Reminder

A beautiful Chrome extension to remind you to drink water and track your daily hydration goals.

About This Extension

Daily Water Reminder is a beautiful Chrome extension designed to help you maintain proper hydration throughout your day. Staying hydrated is essential for focus, energy, and overall health, and this extension makes it easy to track your water intake and receive timely reminders to drink water.

This extension runs locally in your browser and helps you build healthy hydration habits with customizable reminders, progress tracking, and achievement systems.

Key Features

  • Smart Water Reminders
  • Personalized Daily Water Goal
  • Water Intake Tracking
  • Statistics & Achievements
  • Fully Customizable
  • Animated Water Cup Visualization
  • Streak System
  • Dark/Light Themes
  • Quick-add buttons (100ml, 250ml, 500ml)
  • Automatic midnight reset

Installation Guide

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for "Daily Water Reminder".

Click the "Add to Chrome" button on the extension page.

Confirm the installation by clicking "Add Extension" in the pop-up.

Once installed, click the water droplet icon in your toolbar to set up your profile.

Configure your daily water goal and reminder preferences to get started.

How to Use

After installation, click the water droplet icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the extension. Set up your profile by entering your weight, activity level, and climate to calculate your personalized daily water goal, or set a custom goal if you prefer.

The extension will send you gentle reminders at your chosen intervals to drink water. When you consume water, click the "+" buttons in the extension to log your intake. Track your progress throughout the day with the animated water cup visualization and view your statistics to maintain your hydration streak.

Customization Options

  • Custom reminder intervals (20, 30, 45, 60, 90 minutes)
  • Enable/disable reminders easily
  • Custom quick-add button amounts
  • Notification sound preferences
  • Dark/light theme selection
