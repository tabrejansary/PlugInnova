Installation Guide

Navigate to the Chrome Web Store and search for "Website TechStack Detector".

Click the "Add to Chrome" button on the extension's store page.

Confirm the installation by selecting "Add Extension" in the confirmation dialog.

The TechStack Detector icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar once installed.

Visit any website and click the extension icon to analyze its technology stack.

How to Use

Simply navigate to any website you want to analyze and click the TechStack Detector icon in your Chrome toolbar. The extension will instantly scan the website and display a detailed report of the technologies detected, organized into categories like Frontend, Backend, CMS, Analytics, and more.

You can expand each category to see specific technologies and tools used by the website. The extension works entirely locally on your device, ensuring your privacy and security.
